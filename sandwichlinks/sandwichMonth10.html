Organised Students & Graduates Q&A meetup
Although there are a number of graduates and student at STFC, there's hardly any avenue for interaction between these two categories of people. The students in particular as a result dont get the opportunity to gain some knowledge/ideas/insights (which might be valuable going back to Uni) from the graduates. I therefore decided I was going to organise a kind of Question and Answer type event where there will be a few graduates as memebers of a panel and the students (about 30 - if every students attends) as the audience, and the idea being the graduates will get asked questions regarding their life before working at RAL - i.e, talk about their final year project, previous placements they've done, etc...and then about their life since getting to RAL - STFC - their graduates schemes, etc.
This event will be taking place in the first week of May!
Powershell Scripts for Administrating Sharepoint 2013
This week, I spent a while investigating and learning about writing powershell scripts, and specifically for administrating a Sharepoint application. I.e creating elements (like content type, columns, etc) on the site using a powershell script and also automating tasks.
