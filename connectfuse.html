Introducing Connectfuse App to your church family

... a modern approach to connecting your church community

I would like to introduce you to an App called Connectfuse - which my team built and we've been using at The Lighthouse Church, Salford.


Connectfuse is an app that organisations (especially churches) can use to create a safe space where members within their community are able to connect.

Ways members can connect...

Once you've been onboarded to the App as a church, members of your church (following registration and admin approval by your own admin) would be able to use Connectfuse App (web, iOS and Android) to connect in a number of ways including:

Car Sharing

Members in your community can ask for lifts to your main church services, and nearest members (drivers) can be notified.

Businesses Noticeboard

Similar to Craigslist, but private to your community - members of your community with businesses can promote their businesses to other members.

Connect groups

If you have small groups (for youth, young adult, or adults), members can see which is closest to where they live, and contact the leader if they wish to attend.

Prayer connect
(Work In Progress)

Members of your community can share prayer requests and others can support in prayers

Testimonies & Gratitudes
(Work In Progress)

Members of your community can share testimonies to encourage others in your community.
Members can also use as a private gratitude journal - keeping track of what they are daily thankful for.
